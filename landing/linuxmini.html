FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION

Here are some of our Frequently Asked Questions. If you have more enquiries, kindly contact us for any additional clarifications

1. Why Linux?

Linux is the foundation for most astute Developers and Administrators, due to the sheer size of Cloud infrastructure running on the Linux platform. With foundation knowledge of Linux, developers can easily transit to desktop and eventually to Server without breaking a sweat

2. Why Mobile?

Mobile has overtaken PC as the personal computing device of choice for most young people around the globe. If we can consume content, we can definitely produce content. TheMobileProf is an effort to enable young people in Africa start writing code on their Mobile, since for most that is the only device thay have access to.

3. What if I have a Laptop?

If you have a Laptop, you'll be required to get a Linux OS installed to be able to fully participate in our classes. If you are wary about installing a new OS, you can gain remote access to a Linux servers, via SSH, and practice from there. On a windows PC, you'll consider using Putty for Secure access.

4. How can I watch past episodes?

Visit our Download section to download recorded videos of our Classes, grouped by weeks. Week 1 - Week 5 are free of charge. But from Week 6, we have specialized classes that cover topics like Shell Scripting, MySQL, GCloud e.t.c.
