FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION

Here are some of our Frequently Asked Questions. If you have more enquiries, kindly contact us for any additional clarifications

1. What is the difference between normal Office and Office 365?

Office 365 is the same thing as the Normal Office, only difference being that it comes with a Cloud account (Microsoft One Drive), which enables you work on your Laptop, on a browser (www.office.com), and on your Mobile with the Same License

2. Can I access documents I created on my Laptop from my Mobile?

With Office 365, you can store your document on the Cloud and access the document from your Mobile phone as long as you login with the same Microsoft Account both on your Laptop and on your Mobile.

3. Can I get a pirated version?

Microsoft Office 365 is a fully packed Application that comes at a very reasonable monthly subscription of about N3,000 per month. And with this one License, you can work from your Laptop, from your Browser, and from your Mobile. Why would you want a cracked Version?

4. Will I get a Certificate?
