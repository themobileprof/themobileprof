About us

A movement, not just a platform

We believe that with the right skills, you can get remarkable productivity out of a humble smartphone. Using the phone as leverage, we are building a workforce of talented developers and capable professionals across Africa—ready to take on the world.

Founded by Samuel Timothy Anyaele, TheMobileProf began in Yaba, Lagos, teaching students who had no access to laptops. Today we deliver structured courses through our Academy while continuing to partner with NGOs and communities on the ground.
