Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between SEO and SEM?
Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is an optimization technique that ensures that your website ranks organically on Search Engines, while Search Engine Marketing is advertising on Search Engines like Google, Yahoo, and Bing
What is the difference between digital marketing and online marketing?
Digital marketing and online marketing are the same, though technically, Digital marketing encompasses marketing beyond the Internet, while Online Marketing is restricted to the Internet
What is the difference between Social Media Management and Social Media Marketing?
Social Media Marketing / Advertising is sometimes marketed as Social Media Management, but simply put, any strategy that requires you paying money to a platform owner or an influencer is Social Media Marketing. Managing pages, posts, and comments on a Social media platform qualify as Management

How can I advertise on other people's website?
